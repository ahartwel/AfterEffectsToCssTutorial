In the next couple of pages I will show you step by step how to take an animation in After Effects, feed the keyframes into processing, convert it into CSS code, and then implement it into your site, epub, or whatever you are putting your html in.
  • Step 1: Take you artwork in Photoshop and split it up so that each element that you want to animate is on its own layer.
  • Step 2: Go to File>Scripts>Export Layers to Files
  • Step 3: Choose your destination(where you want to save the layers), choose a naming Prefix, and then chose PNG-24 as your file type
  • Step 4: Click Run and your files will save wherever you chose to save them to.
